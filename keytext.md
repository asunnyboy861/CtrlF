## Description
CtrlF brings the power of text search to the physical world. Point your iPhone at any book, document, or printed material, type the word or phrase you are looking for, and watch as matches are highlighted on screen in real time.

No more scanning page after page trying to find that one quote, reference, or keyword. CtrlF uses advanced on-device OCR technology to recognize text through your camera and highlight every match instantly.

KEY FEATURES

Real-Time Text Search
Point your camera at any printed text and type to search. Matches appear instantly with bright yellow highlights, so you can spot what you need at a glance.

Multi-Word Phrase Matching
Search for exact phrases, not just single words. Find "quantum mechanics" or "United States" as complete phrases across any page.

On-Device Processing
All text recognition happens entirely on your device. No internet required. No data sent to servers. Your privacy is fully protected.

Flashlight Support
Working in a dim library or cafe? Toggle the built-in flashlight to illuminate the page without leaving the app.

Search History
Quickly revisit your recent searches. Your search history is stored locally and always just one tap away.

Multi-Language OCR
Supports text recognition in over 25 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

Pro Upgrade
Unlock unlimited searches, custom highlight colors, screenshot capture, audio feedback, and more with a one-time purchase.

Students scanning textbooks for key terms and definitions. Researchers locating specific passages in reference books. Readers finding quotes to share or revisit. Professionals searching through printed reports and documents. Anyone who has ever wished they could Ctrl+F in real life.
